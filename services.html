WE OFFER DIFFERENT SERVICES

We provide a variety of services to both company and personal cars. At Mid City Auto Repairs, no job is too small and we take pride in every job that we do. Our open, honest communication and fair pricing means that we’ll only recommend fixes and repairs that are necessary to ensure your car is roadworthy and safe to drive.

Get In Touch

Engine Diagnostics

Our skilled technicians use advanced diagnostic tools and equipment to quickly identify any issues with your engine and related systems, ensuring you stay on the road safely and reliably.

WOF & COF CHECKS

At Mid City Auto Repair, we provide comprehensive WOF (Warrant of Fitness) and COF (Certificate of Fitness) checks to ensure that your vehicle is roadworthy and fully compliant with safety regulations.

Oil Change

Regular oil changes are essential for maintaining the health of your engine and ensuring optimal performance. Our experienced mechanics use high-quality oils and filters to extend the life of your engine, improve fuel efficiency, and prevent costly repairs down the road.

Breaks

We offer comprehensive brake services designed to keep your braking system in top condition. Whether you’re hearing strange noises, experiencing a soft pedal, or simply want a routine inspection, our expert mechanics are here to help.

Tyre Balancing

We provide expert tyre balancing services to ensure your vehicle’s tires wear evenly and perform optimally. Whether you're experiencing vibrations while driving or simply want to keep your car in top condition, our professional tyre balancing service will help improve your vehicle's handling, extend the life of your tires, and enhance overall safety.
